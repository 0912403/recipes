Ingredients:
 - Sugar: number of sugar spoons equals with number of kilogram meats.
 - Milk: equals with 2/3 sugar.
 - MSG: number of MSG spoons equals with 1/2 number of kilogram meats.
 - Salt: equals with 1/5 MSG.
 - Fish sauce: equals with 1/2 MSG.
 - Annatto seed oil: equals with MSG.
 - Citronella: a little.
 - Onion, garlic, chili finely chopped: depend on your favour.

Directions:
  1. Mix all ingredients and wait for 2-3 hours.
  2. Roast.
